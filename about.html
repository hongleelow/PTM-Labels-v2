About PTM Labels

Over two decades of precision label manufacturing, backed by ISO certification and a commitment to innovation.

Our Story

Manufacturing Labels That Build Brands

Since 2002, PTM Labels has been helping businesses across Malaysia and the Asia Pacific region bring their products to life with high-quality label solutions.

From our facility in Ulu Tiram, Johor, we serve industries ranging from food & beverage and pharmaceuticals to cosmetics, electronics, and household goods. Whether it's a small production run or a multinational rollout, our team delivers with precision and care.

Operating 24 hours a day with both digital and flexographic press technology, we combine the flexibility of modern printing with the reliability of two decades of hands-on experience.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide innovative labelling solutions that contribute to the optimum benefits in product identification, image, and shelf-appeal — while maintaining a commitment to continual improvement, environmental care, and strong partnerships with our customers.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative label management solutions in the Asia Pacific region — continuously growing our market share and financial return by serving all major market segments with excellence.

How We Work

Our Approach to Every Project

Three principles guide how we partner with clients — from first consultation to final delivery.

Understanding Labels

We bring deep industry-specific knowledge to every project. Our team understands the unique labelling requirements across food, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and industrial sectors.

Label Solutions

From adhesive labels and shrink sleeves to variable data and peel-and-reveal formats, we match the right label technology to your product, market, and budget.

Label Innovation

Need something custom? We develop creative, out-of-the-box solutions — including special finishes, security features, and interactive labels that help your products stand out.
